# Hireloom Website Master Prompt

## Role
Act as a world-class product designer, creative director, senior UX strategist, conversion copywriter, and senior frontend engineer. Your task is to design and build the **marketing website** for an AI-first hiring SaaS called **Hireloom**.

This is **not** a generic AI landing page. It should feel like a premium B2B SaaS comparable to Linear, Vercel, Stripe, Notion, Ashby, Greenhouse, Lever, Screenz.ai, Remote, and modern enterprise software.

---

# Brand

- Product Name: **Hireloom**
- Brand Color: **#8028E4**
- Typography: **Aspekta** (or a similar geometric modern grotesk)
- Tone: Premium, intelligent, trustworthy, enterprise-ready
- Audience:
  - HR Leaders
  - Talent Acquisition Teams
  - Startups
  - Mid-market companies
  - Enterprise recruiting teams

I will provide the **official Hireloom logo**. Use that logo consistently throughout the website and derive spacing, clear space, favicon, and header branding from it.

Do NOT create a website that looks AI generated.

- excessive glowing gradients

- generic AI robots
- cheesy holograms

- Features

5. Solution

- Video Pre-screening

- ATS Pipeline

- Security & Compliance

11. Testimonials & Case Studies

12. Pricing

13. FAQ

14. Final CTA

15. Rich Footer
